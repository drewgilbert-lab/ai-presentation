# The "Do Nothing" Path Is Active, Not Passive

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-8 mt-6">
  <div>
    <div class="text-[14px] mb-4">The campaign is not competing against inertia. It is competing against a Director with limited engineering resources being asked by a CFO or board, "could you build this with AI?"</div>
    <ul class="text-[14px]">
      <li>"Questioning whether we need a tool at all"</li>
      <li>"The control element"</li>
      <li>"If we could make it faster in house, that is when I would push for it"</li>
      <li>"It gets you in the ballpark" (chronic-tolerance language)</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  <div class="border-l-4 border-hg-purple bg-hg-light pl-6 py-4 rounded-r-md">
    <div class="font-bold text-hg-navy text-[16px] mb-2">Brief implication</div>
    <div class="text-[14px] text-hg-dark mb-3">The campaign needs an asset that quantifies what an internal AI build actually costs. Engineering time, model-tuning labor, validation cycles, opportunity cost of distracted Mkt Ops.</div>
    <div class="text-[14px] text-hg-dark">No such asset exists in any pulled transcript. This is the production gap.</div>
